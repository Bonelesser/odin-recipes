Brownie

Back to main

Ingredients:

  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Cocoa
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate chunks

Steps:

  1. Line parchment paper to a baking pan
  2. Melt together chocolate and butter in a bowl and mix
  3. Whisk in eggs
  4. Add in flour and cocoa powder and mix
  5. Stir in chocolate chunks
  6. Scrape into lined pan and bake for 28 minutes
