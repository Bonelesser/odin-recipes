Steak

Description

A steak recipe. Simple and tasty.

Back to main

Ingredients:

  • Strip steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Rosemary sprigs
  • Garlic cloves

Steps:

  1. Pat the steak dry
  2. Sprinkle both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat the pan on medium and brush with oil. Using just 1/2 Tbsp oil reduces splatter.
  4. Add steak and sear each side 3-4 minutes until a brown crust has formed then use tongs to turn steaks on their sides and sear edges (1 min per edge)
  5. Melt in butter with quartered garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs.
  6. Remove steak and rest 10 minutes before slicing against the grain, then serve
